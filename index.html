and here's how to use it:
<div class="grid">
	<div class="row">
		<div class="column six">
			hella.
		</div>
	</div>
</div>
  • grid is the 960px column container. Every grid related thing goes in here.
  • row is the horizontal container. Columns that need to sit next to other columns go in these. Has a maximum width of twelve.
  • column is your column.
  • six is the width of the column. Choose between two and twelve. You don't need to define a width if it's just one.
  • offset-six applies a margin of six to the left of the column. Choose from one to eleven. Optional.
  • non-responsive prevents the column from resizing or losing its offset on small screens. Optional.
